"RESEARCH AND INNOVATION: A CONFLUENCE OF SCIENCE & TECHNOLOGY"

About The Symposium

In active support of Department of Research and Innovation, The Department of Civil Engineering is planning to organize a 1st Student Symposium on RESEARCH AND INNOVATION: CONFLUENCE OF TECH AND NON-TECH for all MUJ students of postgraduate and doctoral level on 8th February 2019. The proposed symposium has been aimed to inculcate the research and entrepreneurship offered by various department of MUJ to present and disseminate our students with intuitive academic thoughts on intellect based platforms. Furthermore, considering the prevailing highly competitive professional scenario in the open global market, it is quite imperative and warranting to develop top notch research and its presentation skills in our perspective students. It is also pertinent to mention that our students would ultimately become an effective influential pocket in their global profession as well as academic world and also reinforcing the vision of Manipal University Jaipur i.e. “Global Leadership in Human Development, Excellence in Education and Healthcare”. Getting the motivation from Faculty Development Program (FDP) conducted by our Chairperson, MUJ (Prof. (Dr.) K. Ramanarayan), it is proposed that a Student Development Program (SDP) should also be initiated at university levels in order to facilitate regular impetus on development of professional and academic skills amongst our perspective students. Moreover, the proposed symposium will provide a common platform to students of all Department to present and share their proposed thoughts and research endeavors among each other. The expertise level of the individual speakers in different fields is expected to synergize development of inter-disciplinary ideas in university.

   Objectives Of Symposium

	  The proposed activities is endeavored to fulfil the following benefiting objectives and 
	  impacts from student point of view:
	1)	To provide a forum for students to present their scholarly, creative and research
		work to the university community.
	2)	To provide a platform to students to improve their research and technical
		presentation skills.
	3)	To create an opportunity for PG and PhD students and motivate them suitably
		for futureand possible collaboration in field of research and innovation
		among the various department/braches within MUJ.
	4)	To develop a relation between students of different years and branches; and to create 
		conducive working environment within the university.
	5)	To promote the “Student Development Program (SDP)” in the university. 
	6)	To establish the guidelines to improve the research environment and exchanges program.
